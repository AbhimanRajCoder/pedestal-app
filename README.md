# Pedestal - 🚀 Gamified Financial Literacy & Investing Simulator

Welcome to **Pedestal**, the ultimate modern, Gen-Z focused financial education app designed to make learning about money addictive, interactive, and completely risk-free. 

Pedestal combines a premium Neo-Brutalist aesthetic with powerful gamification features, wrapping complex financial topics inside bite-sized micro-lessons, AI-powered assistance, and realistic market simulators.

---

## 🔥 Key Features

### 1. 📚 Gamified Learning Roadmap (Duolingo Style)
Master personal finance through an expansive, interactive learning tree.
*   **Micro-lessons:** Bite-sized chapters categorized into topics like Personal Finance, Investing, and Savings.
*   **Swipeable Flashcards:** Learn new terms playfully with front-and-back animated flashcards.
*   **Interactive Quizzes:** Test your knowledge. Wrong answers are seamlessly added back into the learning queue so you truly master the topic before advancing.
*   **Progress Tracking:** Unlock chapters, earn XP, and track your proficiency.

### 2. 📈 Paper Trading & Market Simulator
Experience the thrill of trading without losing a dime.
*   **Virtual Portfolio:** Start with ₹1,00,000 in virtual cash.
*   **Live Neo-Brutalist Dashboard:** Track your "Live Virtual Net Worth" and P&L at a glance.
*   **Stock Discovery:** Explore a hardcoded, hyper-realistic market of tech, banking, and energy stocks.

### 3. ⚔️ Market Arena (Historical Crisis Simulator)
Learn from the past so you don't repeat it in the future.
*   **Crisis Simulation:** Get dropped into a historical market event (like the 2008 Financial Crash or the 2020 COVID Drop).
*   **Allocation Challenge:** Decide how to distribute your capital across different asset classes during peak volatility.
*   **Day-by-Day Progression:** Step through historical days and watch how your customized portfolio reacts to the real-world panic.

### 4. 🤖 "Talk with Pedestal" (AI Financial Guide)
Your personal pocket CFO.
*   **Conversational AI:** A dedicated assistant designed to answer complex financial questions easily.
*   **Contextual Advice:** Provides actionable steps and explanations tailored for complete beginners.

### 5. 🏆 Diamond League Leaderboard
Compete with your peers and rise to the top.
*   **Global Podiums:** A visually stunning podium showcasing the top three learners.
*   **League Tiers:** Rank up and earn visual badges indicating your mastery level.

### 6. 🔥 Streaks & Daily Check-ins
Consistency is key to wealth.
*   **Streak Tracking:** A massive visual flame tracks your daily learning streak.
*   **Weekly Heatmap:** A visual calendar displaying the days you successfully studied.
*   **Streak Freezes & Milestones:** Earn streak freezes to protect your progress and hit major milestones (7-day, 30-day, 100-day).
*   **9 PM Nightly Check-ins:** Reflect on your daily spending habits actively.

## 🎨 Design System
Pedestal is built using a custom **Neo-Brutalist** design framework aiming for a premium, modern feel:
*   Bold typography and heavy borders.
*   Harsh, deep drop shadows.
*   Vibrant and energetic Gen-Z color palettes (Neon Greens, Pastel Purples, Deep Navy).
*   Rich micro-interactions, haptic feedback, and sound effects for every major action (flipping cards, correct answers, streak increments).

---

## 💻 Tech Stack
*   **Frontend**: React Native, Expo, Expo Router
*   **Styling**: Custom CSS/StyleSheet focusing on brutalist properties
*   **Icons & Assets**: Lucide React Native, React Native SVG
*   **State & Storage**: React Hooks, AsyncStorage for offline progression
*   **Backend** (Complementary AI/Data Services): FastAPI, Python, Supabase

---

## 🚀 Getting Started

1. **Clone the repository**
2. **Navigate to the frontend**
   ```bash
   cd frontend/pedestal
   ```
3. **Install Dependencies**
   ```bash
   npm install
   ```
4. **Run the App**
   ```bash
   npx expo start
   ```
   *Press `i` for iOS simulator or `a` for Android emulator.*
